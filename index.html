Introducing Treats

Pay it friendly

Sign up with the app and get your Treats Visa Prepaid Card in the mail.

Use like your normal debit card to buy everyday stuff,

Every 7 purchases create a Treat to give to a friend.

When you open a Treat from your friend, it may pay for your purchase.

Check out friends’ Treats in the Newsfeed. Boost Treats you like to help them win.

Pay any Treats member instantly through the app. No fees.

Your app keeps track of every card swipe and payment to a friend.

Treats has no overdraft fees, no monthly fees, and no fee to get your card. That's how we like it.
